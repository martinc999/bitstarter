Many things depend on reliable and verified information. Some of it can be really critical. Imagine what may happen if the jury reaches a verdict based on false testimony. The truth recognizer can address this problem. With google glasses and our software you can recognize all the unexpected microexpressions connected with a particular context. Typical contexts include: giving testimony, negotiating, having a serious discussion with your children, investigating a crime, and more. Based on the context, you will be able to notice all the suspicious microexpressions on your interlocutor's face and to recognize if he/she is lying to you.

Thanks to high resolution of the camera built in Google glasses we can analyze all the microexpressions appearing on human face. Those data can be analyzed and, then, they can give information about facial expressions connected with the verbal content. On the basis of these emotions (facial expressions) we can tell whether or not a given emotion (expression) is coherent with the content of the utterance. Our system can provide you with that information in a very useful manner using specially prepared emoticons.

Market: jury in courts, negotiators, police officers, human resources departments (potentially hundreds of thousends of users).
